Note:
The camera's internal clock claimed it was morning (06:10 AM), which would mean the sun was rising in the East (behind the mountains).
However, the embedded GPS Time is recorded by satellites in UTC (05:10).
Converting this to Hawaii time (05:10 - 10 hours) yields 19:10 (7:10 PM).
The Result: At 7:10 PM, the sun is setting in the West (~288°), which matches
the filename ("sunset") and the location (Kaanapali Beach).
